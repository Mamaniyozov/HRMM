# Ikki Ton mavzusi qo'shildi

## Nima o'zgardi

- `themes.js` ga yangi `ikki-ton` mavzusi qo'shildi.
- `styles.css` da mavzuga mos body fon gradientlari, to'q indigo sidebar, login chap paneli va primary button hover transformi qo'shildi.
- `app.js` da theme preview SVG chap panelni `rail` rangi bilan chizadigan qilindi.

## Ranglar

- Fon: `#eef1f6` (deep: `#e4e8f0`, elevated: `#f4f6fb`)
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Sidebar: `#171a2e`
- Aksent: `#5b6cff` ‚Üí `#2dd4bf` (gradient)
- Matn: `#1d2333` (muted: `#7c86a0`)
- Chegara (`border`) minimal qoldi (`rgba(163, 171, 194, 0.35)`).
